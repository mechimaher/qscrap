At QScrap, we believe in fair and transparent refund policies. This document outlines when and how you can receive a refund for your orders. Our escrow payment system ensures your money is protected throughout the entire transaction process.

1

7-Day Return Guarantee

All parts purchased through QScrap come with a 7-day return guarantee from the date of delivery:

  • If the part doesn't fit your vehicle as specified
  • If the part is significantly different from the description
  • If the part is defective or damaged upon receipt
  • If the part was incorrectly identified by the garage

Full refund guaranteed within 7 days if the part doesn't match the listing description or is defective.

2

Cancellation Stages & Refunds

Your refund amount depends on when you cancel your order:

Stage When Refund
Before Garage Accepts Order submitted, waiting for garage 100% Refund
After Garage Accepts Garage confirmed, preparing part 100% Refund
Part Ready Part prepared, awaiting pickup 90% Refund
Driver Assigned Driver picked up the part 85% Refund
In Transit Part is on the way to you 80% Refund
After Delivery Within 7-day return window 85-100% Refund*

*Note: Post-delivery refunds may vary based on part condition. Full refund for defective/wrong parts. Return shipping may be deducted for change-of-mind returns.

3

Non-Refundable Items

The following situations are not eligible for refund:

  • Parts that have been installed or modified
  • Parts damaged after delivery due to customer handling
  • Parts returned after the 7-day return window
  • Custom-order or specially sourced parts (unless defective)
  • Electrical parts that have been tested/connected (unless defective on arrival)

Important: Always inspect your parts before installation. Once installed, parts cannot be returned for fitment issues.

4

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Open the QScrap app and go to "My Orders"
  • Select the order you wish to return or cancel
  • Tap "Request Refund" and select your reason
  • Upload photos if reporting a defective or wrong part
  • Our support team will review within 24 hours

For urgent issues, contact us directly at support@qscrap.qa or WhatsApp +974 5026 7974.

5

Refund Processing Time

  • Card Payments: 5-7 business days after approval
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 3-5 business days
  • QScrap Wallet: Instant credit after approval

Tip: Refunds to your QScrap Wallet are processed instantly and can be used for future purchases or withdrawn to your bank account.

6

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Request an escalation through the app or email
  • Our senior support team will review within 48 hours
  • You may provide additional evidence (photos, videos)
  • Final decisions comply with Qatar Consumer Protection Law

Consumer Rights: Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under Qatar's Consumer Protection Law (Law No. 8 of 2008) and MOCI regulations.
