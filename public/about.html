Our Mission

We're on a mission to revolutionize how Qatar sources automotive parts. By connecting customers directly with verified garages, we eliminate the middlemen, reduce costs, and ensure quality parts reach you faster.

Our platform handles everything from discovery to delivery, providing a seamless experience backed by Qatar's strongest consumer protections including a 7-day return guarantee and secure escrow payments.

Whether you need a rare OEM part or a standard replacement, QScrap connects you with the right garage in minutes, not days.
