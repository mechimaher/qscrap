2

Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use our Apps, we collect:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (optional), preferred language
  • Vehicle Information: VIN (Vehicle Identification Number), make, model, year for parts matching
  • Payment Information: Payment method preferences (Cash on Delivery, Card)
  • Business Information (for Garages): Commercial Registration number, bank account details (IBAN), business address
  • Driver Information: Qatar ID, driver's license, vehicle registration, bank account for payouts

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Apps, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, push notification tokens
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for delivery zone calculation, real-time tracking during deliveries
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, browsing patterns, search queries, session duration
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, system activity
  • Photos and Media: Images you upload for part requests (VIN photos, part images, proof of delivery)

2.3 Location Data

Important: Our Apps require location access to function properly. We use location data for:

  • Calculating accurate delivery fees based on distance
  • Matching you with nearby garages
  • Real-time order tracking and delivery coordination
  • Driver navigation and route optimization

You may disable location services in your device settings, but this will significantly impact the functionality of our services including delivery fee calculation and order tracking.

3

How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Processing and fulfilling spare part requests and orders
  • Facilitating bidding between customers and garages
  • Calculating delivery fees based on your location
  • Providing real-time order tracking and notifications
  • Processing payments and payouts
  • Managing warranties, returns, and refunds

3.2 Communication

  • Sending order confirmations, updates, and delivery notifications
  • Customer support and dispute resolution
  • Important service announcements and policy updates
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)

3.3 Improvement & Safety

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Ensuring platform safety and security
  • Compliance with legal obligations
4

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

4.1 Service Partners

  • Garages: Your name, phone number, and delivery address for order fulfillment
  • Drivers: Your name, phone number, and delivery location for order delivery
  • Payment Processors: Transaction details for payment processing

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS/Google Cloud for secure data storage
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage analysis
  • Maps & Location: Google Maps for location services
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by Qatar law, court orders, or government regulations, including compliance with Qatar's Law No. 13 of 2016 on Personal Data Protection.

5

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in encrypted databases with access controls
  • Authentication: OTP-based verification for account access
  • Access Control: Role-based access restrictions for employees
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Incident Response: Procedures for data breach notification within 72 hours

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and fulfill orders
  • Comply with Qatar's legal requirements (10-year retention for commercial records per Qatar Commercial Law)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain audit trails for regulatory compliance

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7

Your Rights

Under Qatar's data protection laws and our policies, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a common format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions for optional data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@qscrap.qa. We will respond within 30 days.

8

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion through the app or by contacting us. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile and personal data will be removed or anonymized
  • Order history may be retained in anonymized form for legal/audit purposes
  • Some data required by law (invoices, transaction records) will be retained per Qatar Commercial Law
9

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

10

Third-Party Links

Our Apps may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside Qatar (e.g., cloud infrastructure in the UAE, Europe, or USA). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with Qatar's Law No. 13 of 2016.

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to your registered email address
  • A prominent notice on our website

Continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Qatar, including Law No. 13 of 2016 on Personal Data Protection. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Qatar courts.
